About Us

Empowering People and Businesses Through Technology

SyntraSoft is a forward-thinking technology company providing innovative solutions in software development, hardware repair, IT consulting, and tech supply services. Built on the belief that synergy between people and technology creates limitless possibilities, we deliver modern, reliable, and scalable digital solutions that help individuals and organizations thrive in a connected world.

2015

Started as a small tech service focused on fast and reliable hardware repairs for local clients.

2017

Expanded into full IT support, including diagnostics, networking, data recovery, and system optimization.

2020

Introduced software development, website creation, and custom digital solutions for businesses.

2024

Evolved into SyntraSoft a full technology consulting and IT procurement company trusted by individuals, SMEs, and organizations.
